Memorize License Plates

(California)

A random California license plate will be shown.
Memorize the plate number.
You may backspace and enter your own number.
Then click 'Got It' and enter the plate number.
You may enter lowercase or uppercase letters.
After you have memorized the number, click 'Verify'.
Click the Show button to see the correct license characters.
Click New Plate to generate a new plate.
